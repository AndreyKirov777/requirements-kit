# Compliance Officer Clara

## Overview
Clara is the primary user responsible for monitoring battery passport compliance with EU 2023/1542. She works as a Compliance Officer at an EV battery manufacturer.

## Demographics
- **Age Range**: 35–50
- **Tech Level**: Medium
- **Frequency of Use**: Daily

## Goals
- Track completeness of all battery passports across the fleet
- Identify missing data before regulatory deadlines
- Generate compliance reports for regulatory authorities
- Demonstrate readiness for regulatory audits

## Pain Points
- No single view of passport completeness
- Passport data scattered across multiple systems
- Unclear which fields are regulation-mandatory vs optional
- Manual coordination with operations teams

## Behaviors
- Checks compliance dashboard daily
- Exports reports weekly for regulatory reviews
- Escalates missing data issues to operations team
- Proactively prepares audit documentation
